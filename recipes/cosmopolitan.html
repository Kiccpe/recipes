Cosmopolitan

Description

This is a recipe for a Cosmopolitan cocktail

If you don't have cranberry juice, use cherry juice instead

If and only if you are Kiccpe, add the FIZZ

Ingredients

  • 2 shots of Vodka
  • 2 shots of cranberry juice
  • 1 shot of triple sec or cointreau
  • lime juice
  • ice

Preparation Steps

  1. place all the ingredients into your cocktail shaker
  2. shake generously and strain into a martini glass
  3. dont decorate cause that shit is pointless
