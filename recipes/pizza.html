Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a pizza that we wholeheartedly recommend you top with some mozzarella and pepperonis

This recipe will contain a how to make the dough, but also how to properly bake it in the oven

Ingredients

Dough

  • 540g strong white bread flour
  • 325g water
  • 16g of salt
  • 1g of yeast
  • A pinch of sugar

For the Sauce

  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • a generous drizzle of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • a pinch of salt
  • a pinch of sugar
  • 2teaspoons of Oregano

Toppings

  • Mozzarella cheese, sliced to circles
  • pepperoni
  • jalapenos
  • whatever your heart desires, as long as it is NOT FRUIT

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing all of the dough ingredients
  2. Whip up the sauce, let it simmer for 10 minutes on medium heat
  3. cut up your mozzarella cheese and your jalapenos
  4. Knead the dough and roll it out on top of your pizza peel
  5. Bake the pizza at maximum oven heat for approximately 10 minutes , rotate after 5 minutes
